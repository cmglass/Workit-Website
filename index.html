About Us

workIt

for individuals who struggle to stay in shape, workIt provides a fitness application to guide individuals on what to do to get started with working out, and on how to do it by helping them perfect their form and technique.

Self-customizing

The Workit system uses machine learning techniques to learn your specific needs and adapt the workouts it suggests to suit them. Over time the app will learn how fast you recover from being sore, how easily you build strength, how hard you want to push yourself and your favorite exercises. All associated with your profile to give you a partner in training.

Ramp-up guidance

The app increases difficulty as fast or as slow as you need to keep your workouts effective and interesting. It uses benchmarks to determine where your comfort level is and then slowly over time introduces new techniques, workouts, and styles to help you meet your workout goals.

Technique Assistance

Many apps tell you what to do workIt also tells you how. Every exercise it suggests is linked to a description and video of the exercise to give accurate and complete guidance. Additionally it assists you in taking a video of yourself doing it to compare your form to an experts.
